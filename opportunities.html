Career Opportunities

Explore job openings and career paths in cybersecurity

Red Team

Penetration Tester

Conduct authorized security assessments on client systems. Identify vulnerabilities and provide detailed reports with remediation recommendations. Requires 2+ years of experience with penetration testing tools and methodologies.

Experience Level: Intermediate | Salary: $80,000 - $120,000

Red Team

Security Researcher

Research new vulnerabilities, develop exploits, and contribute to the security community. Publish findings and present at conferences. Requires strong programming skills and deep understanding of system internals.

Experience Level: Advanced | Salary: $100,000 - $150,000

Blue Team

Security Operations Center (SOC) Analyst

Monitor security alerts, investigate incidents, and respond to threats in real-time. Analyze logs and security events using SIEM platforms. Entry-level position perfect for starting your security career.

Experience Level: Entry | Salary: $50,000 - $70,000

Blue Team

Incident Response Specialist

Lead incident investigations, coordinate response efforts, and develop remediation strategies. Conduct forensic analysis and create incident reports. Requires 3+ years of security experience.

Experience Level: Intermediate | Salary: $90,000 - $130,000

Blue Team

Security Architect

Design secure infrastructure, implement security policies, and oversee enterprise security programs. Requires 5+ years of security experience and strong architectural knowledge.

Experience Level: Advanced | Salary: $120,000 - $180,000

Red Team

Malware Analyst

Analyze malware samples, reverse engineer binaries, and develop detection signatures. Contribute to threat intelligence and help organizations defend against malware attacks.

Experience Level: Intermediate | Salary: $85,000 - $125,000

Internships & Entry-Level Programs

Start your cybersecurity career with these programs

Security Internship Programs

Many Fortune 500 companies and startups offer paid internships in cybersecurity. These programs provide hands-on experience and mentorship from industry professionals.

Graduate Trainee Programs

Structured programs for recent graduates with intensive training, rotations across different teams, and clear career progression paths.

Apprenticeships

Combine work and training to develop practical skills while earning. Many organizations offer apprenticeships in cybersecurity and IT.

Bug Bounty Programs

Earn money by finding vulnerabilities in companies' systems. Platforms like HackerOne and Bugcrowd connect researchers with organizations.

Research & Collaboration Opportunities

Contribute to the security community and advance the field

Vulnerability Disclosure

Discover vulnerabilities and responsibly disclose them to vendors. Many organizations have bug bounty programs that reward researchers for finding and reporting security issues.

Open Source Security Projects

Contribute to open-source security tools and projects. Help improve tools like Metasploit, Burp Suite extensions, and other community-driven projects.

Security Research Papers

Publish your research findings in academic journals and conferences. Share your discoveries with the security community and establish yourself as an expert.

Mentorship Programs

Mentor junior security professionals and students. Share your knowledge and help others grow in the cybersecurity field.

Competitions & Challenges

Test your skills and compete with other security professionals

Capture The Flag (CTF)

Compete in CTF competitions to solve security challenges and capture flags. Participate in online CTFs or attend in-person competitions.

Hackathons

Join security-focused hackathons to build security tools, solve real-world problems, and network with other professionals.

Bug Bounty Competitions

Participate in time-limited bug bounty competitions where you can earn rewards for finding vulnerabilities.

Red Team Exercises

Participate in authorized red team exercises to test your offensive security skills in realistic scenarios.

Cyber Defense Competitions

Compete in blue team competitions where you defend systems against simulated attacks.

Wargames & Challenges

Solve online wargames and security challenges on platforms like OverTheWire, PicoCTF, and others.

Popular Opportunity Platforms

HackerOne

The world's largest bug bounty platform. Connect with organizations and earn rewards for finding vulnerabilities.

Bugcrowd

Crowdsourced security testing platform. Participate in bug bounty and vulnerability disclosure programs.

TryHackMe

Interactive platform with hands-on labs, challenges, and career paths in cybersecurity.

HackTheBox

Platform with vulnerable machines and challenges to practice penetration testing and security skills.

CTFTime

Aggregator of CTF competitions worldwide. Find and participate in Capture The Flag events.

Search for cybersecurity jobs on LinkedIn, Indeed, and specialized security job boards.

Local Meetups & User Groups
