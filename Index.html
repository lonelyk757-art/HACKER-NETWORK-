Ethical Use Agreement

  • Comply with Laws – Use only where explicitly authorized.
  • Do No Harm – Never disrupt systems, steal data, or exploit vulnerabilities without permission.
  • Educate & Research – Apply tools solely for learning, ethical hacking, or securing systems you own/control.
  • Stay Accountable – Misuse is your responsibility; we ban violators and report illegal activity.

Tools are for defense, education, and ethical practice—not malice.
